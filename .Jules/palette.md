## 2026-06-11 - Enhance form accessibility and feedback
**Learning:** Users benefit from explicit feedback when typing into character-limited fields, and accessible labeling (via 'for' and 'aria-describedby') is crucial for screen reader users to understand the purpose and constraints of inputs.
**Action:** Always ensure inputs have associated labels and provide real-time character counts for limited textareas, using 'aria-live' for screen reader feedback.
